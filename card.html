Cat

A cat's purr may be a form of self-healing, as it can be a sign of nervousness as well as contentment.

  • Scientific Name: Felis catus
  • Type: Mammals
  • Dite: Carnivores
  • Weight: 5 to 20 lbs

Cats have 250 bones in their body, five toes on each front paw, but only four toes on each back paw. They have the largest eyes of any mammal and most cats do not have eyelashes. The AB system is the major blood group system in domestic cats.
